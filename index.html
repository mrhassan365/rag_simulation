How to think about RAG

This is the clean mental model to keep in your head.

Without RAG

The model answers from general memory and learned patterns. It may sound reasonable, but it does not know your specific documents unless that information is provided at question time.

With RAG

The system first searches your external knowledge, retrieves the most relevant chunks, inserts those chunks into the prompt, and then the model answers from that retrieved evidence.

What makes this version better

The difference is now much more concrete.

  • The “without RAG” side now acts like a model that cannot verify specifics.
  • The “with RAG” side breaks the answer into evidence-backed lines.
  • You can click an answer line and see which retrieved chunk supports it.
  • You can test questions that are fully supported, partially supported, or unsupported.
